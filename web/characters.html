CHARACTERS:

REBEL and ROGUE

-Age: 14

Cousins Rebel and Rogue have always been a bit shy, yet they both have a really strong personality. Rebel wants to become a great trainer and that's why she signed up for the gym challenge together with her cousin Rogue. Rogue has a great heart as well, and never leaves his friends behind. He is smart and will help his friends no matter in which problems they are in, while also doing anything to prove himself, even if it includes defeating you in battle.

Sprites:

RAVEN

-Age: 14

-Pokémon team: Riolu

This rude young lady causes nothing but trouble on your journey. She rarely reveals anything about herself, but she seems to be on an adventure as well, whatever her goals may be. Together with her partner Riolu, Raven usually tries to catch Pokémon and sell them afterwards. She doesn't mind others getting hurt and only cares about Riolu.

Professors DAWN and LUCAS

-Age: 23

The famous and beloved Rowan's assitants have grown into a brilliant professors, that will help you around your journey.

Sprites:

Neo Team Galactic Grunts

After the disappearance of their boss, Team Galactic started to fall apart. Many of the former members had the ambition to start a new life and leave Team Galactic behind. However, many grunts felt lost and not welcome in the world their master sought to destroy. Thus, it is no surprise that a large amount of grunts happily rejoined the team, once it started to reorganize itself.

Mars

-Age: 36

-Pokémon team:

Purugly
Houndoom
Crobat

After Charon had proven himself unworthy of being Cyrus' successor, Commander Mars decided to go on a journey, distancing herself from the team. But, unable to forget the man who had given her a place to live and a reason to fight, Mars couldn't start all over again. She began idolizing Cyrus, the only true father figure in her life, and wished for nothing more than his return. Mars was the first member of the organization who rejoined Saturn's newly formed team. Now, she is determined to bring her former master back.
